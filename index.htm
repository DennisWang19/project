Explain the output

As depicted in the correlation heatmap, there is a significant positive correlation between dew point temperature (dewp) and air temperature (temp). This suggests that changes in dew point temperature tend to be associated with corresponding changes in air temperature. However, it’s important to note that correlation does not imply causation, and other factors may also influence the relationship between these variables. Additionally, the strength of the correlation should be considered, with higher correlation coefficients indicating a stronger linear relationship.
